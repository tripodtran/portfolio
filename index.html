Welcome to my Workshop

I’m Sidney Konig-Brock, a fine carpenter focused on sustainable, modern craft. My work blends traditional woodworking techniques with contemporary design, creating timeless pieces rooted in the beauty of natural materials.

I studied at the Kingston School of Arts in London, where I earned a Diploma of Higher Education in Furniture Design, and at the City and Guilds, London, completing a Foundation Degree in Arts — training that has guided over a decade of dedication to art and fine carpentry, during which I’ve created bespoke pieces for renowned clients across two countries and numerous states.
